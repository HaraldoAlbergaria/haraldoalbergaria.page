About Me

I'm a hobbyist photographer from Campinas, Brazil. I like to photograph any subject but I shoot mostly nature and travel photography.

My background is in Computer Science and Electronics and I work as an R&D Specialist in Microelectronics for a multinational company of the Semiconductor industry, using my vacations to travel and photograph.

So far, I visited 32 cities and 10 nature parks in seven countries.

On my spare time, I like to write scripts and build apps related to photography. They are available on my GitHub profile.
